Step 1

Create a user account and login. This involves adding all the required infromation such as your email and cellphone number and any additional resources such as your omang number.

Step 2

After signing into the client section locate the theory test registration form. Fill in all the require details. The system will provide you with suitable dates for writing. The required pass mark is 26/30. If candidates recieves anything below that they fail and therefore have to re-write their theory test.

Step 3

After meeting the pass mark for the theory test student will be allowed to book for their yard/cone test.Candidates will fill the provided form with the information required. The candidate will then visit any yard testing area and perform their test. If student passes they will proceed to register for their road test but if they fail they re-write their yard.

Step 4

Once student has provided proof of passing their yard test they will proceed to register for the road test. Student are required to fill the form the required information. The candidate will once again visit a testing area and perform their tests. If student pass they will proceed to make their license. If student fails they will re-do their road test

Step 5

Once student has passed all their tests they will go to the nearest DRTS with all their tests ( road, yard, theory), omang, and license fee and create their licence.

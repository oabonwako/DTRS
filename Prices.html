Driver License Application Prices

In order to apply for a Botswana National driver's license one must go through a series of 3 tests being. Theory, Yard and Road. for every test an amount is payed to progress to the next test. Lastly, the candidate must pay for the driver's license fee.


Item Cost
Theory Test BWP 52.00
Learner's License BWP 39.00
Yard Test BWP 52.00
Road Test BWP 52.00
Driver's License BWP 91.00
